Esta espacio va dedicado a mi padre Antonio Garcia Nogales.

Nacido en Valencia en 1942 y trabajando desde muy joven debido a la situación financiera de la época, decidió emigrar con su mujer, mi madre, a Bélgica a finales de los anos 60.

Ha trabajado de cestero durante 60 años intentando a la vez ser un buen marido y un buen padre.

Espero que con estas fotos le rinda homenaje a su duro trabajo y bella artesanía.

Claro esta que sin la ayuda y el apoyo de mi madre Maria Luisa Algarra no hubiese llegado al nivel de artesano que ha logrado. Un beso para los dos.

I would like to dedicate this space to my father.

He has been working as a willow basket maker for 60 years now. He was born in Valencia in early 40's and started working much too soon due to the financial situation in that period. He decided to move to Belgium with my mother to build up more financial security as a craftsman.

I hope these few pictures that I have taken from him can render a bit of an homage to his hard work.

Of course, it goes without saying that he wouldn't have obtained this level of craftsmanship without the help and support of my mother.
