shooting weddings

In Wedding Photography, I strive to document the event as if I were not there, trying not to interfere, just let it happen, capture the moments and the atmosphere on black & white film or digital sensor. This reportage style will give you a very different and intriguing look on your wedding day, a look that you will cherish for a lifetime and beyond.

Before the wedding day, I meet the couple one or two times, to get to know each other a bit, talk, take some pictures and as a result break the eventual ice and "camera shyness".

My photographic tools consist of 2 Leica film cameras, 1 Leica Leica camera and some prime Leica lenses as well as two medium format cameras, Hasselblad SWC/M and Rolleiflex 2.8 TLR.

The resulting wedding album is usually designed and edited by myself in colloboration with you.

For Film Photography (analogue), I develop and enlarge the negatives by myself or, depending on the circumstances, I entrust work to the darkroom specialist I've been working with for the last 4 years here in Hiroshima. I offer different qualities of paper (RC paper or Baryte paper) and different sizes for such analogue print.

For Digital Photography, the prints are made in a professional digital print labo also in Hiroshima. Of course, a Digital Book is also possible.

If you want to know more or if you want a price quote, please don't hesitate to contact me.
